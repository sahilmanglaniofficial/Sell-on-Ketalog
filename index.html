List Products

What is a listing? A listing refers to the process of registering your product on the Ketalog platform, making it visible to customers, and enabling them to view and purchase your product. It involves creating a detailed product page that includes essential information such as product title, description, images, pricing, and other relevant details.

A well-crafted listing helps attract potential customers and facilitates the sale of your product on Ketalog.

Did you know that providing precise and comprehensive information about your product, along with clear and captivating images, can increase its visibility on our platform by up to 15%.

Storage & Shipping

Congratulations on receiving your first order! When it comes to shipping your products to customers, Ketalog understands the importance of fast and reliable delivery in secure packaging.

We provide two fulfillment options for you to choose from

✓ Fulfilment by Ketalog (FBK)
✓ Non Fulfilment by Ketalog (NFBK)

Consider the fulfillment option that best suits your needs and preferences to ensure timely and secure delivery of your products to Ketalog customers.

Fulfilment by Ketalog (FBK)

Eliminate worries about storage, packing, shipping, and delivery by leveraging Ketalog's Fulfilment by Ketalog (FBK) service.FBF offers a comprehensive solution that handles all your shipping needs under one roof. With FBF, you can entrust ketalog to efficiently manage the entire process, from storing your products to expertly packing and shipping them to customers. Enjoy a hassle-free experience and focus on growing your business while Ketalog takes care of the logistics.

✓Pick-up from seller location to Ketalog warehouse
✓KAssured badge
✓Faster delivery to customers
✓Seamless order processing
✓Customer returns handled end-to-end
✓Warehouse space availability
✓Quality selection recommendations
✓Rigorous quality checks
✓Quality packaging materials

Non Fulfilment by Ketalog (NFBK)

With Non-Fulfillment by Ketalog (NFBF), you can benefit from end-to-end delivery of your products directly from your location to the customer. In NFBF, it is the responsibility of the seller to ensure that the product is properly packed and ready for dispatch before the ketalog agent arrives at the seller's location. This service allows you to maintain control over the packaging process while leveraging ketalog's logistics network for efficient and reliable delivery.

✓Delivery to 19000+ pin codes across India
✓Tracking of your product
✓Customer returns support
✓Logistics support from community warehouse available
